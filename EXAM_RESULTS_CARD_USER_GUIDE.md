# Exam Results Portal Card - User Guide

## Overview

The **Exam Results Card** is now integrated into the Parent Portal Dashboard, making it easy for parents and students to check exam results instantly.

## Where to Find It

### For Parents:
1. Log into **Parent Portal**
2. Select your child's name (if you have multiple children)
3. Click the **"Exams"** tab

### For Students:
1. Log into your **Student Portal**
2. The exam results card will be visible on your dashboard

## How to Use the Card

### Step 1: Access the Tab
- Tap or click the **"Exams"** tab in the Parent Portal

### Step 2: View Your Results
- See all your published exam results
- Most recent results appear first

### Step 3: Expand for Details
- **Click** on any exam result to see:
  - All subject grades
  - Exam status (Published, Pending, etc.)
  - When the results were released

### Step 4: View Certificate
- Click **"View Full Certificate"** to:
  - See official exam certificate
  - Print or download results
  - Share with others

## Key Information on the Card

### Session Details
- **Session Name**: E.g., "Final Exam 2024"
- **Year**: Academic year of the exam
- **Level**: Your current school level (e.g., Secondary 4)

### Student Information
- **Index Number**: Your exam registration number
- **Status**: Whether results are published and official

### Grade Information
- **Individual Grades**: Letter grade (A-E) for each subject
- **Overall Division**: Overall performance rating (1-4)

## Important Notes

### Result Status
- Only **Published** results are visible
- Pending results will appear once officially released
- Results may be blocked in certain circumstances (contact school)

### Multiple Exams
- If you have taken multiple exams, they'll all be listed
- Most recent exams appear at the top
- Click to expand any exam to see full details

### No Results Yet?
- If you see "No exam results available yet":
  - Exams may not be marked as published
  - Your exam results may still be pending
  - Contact your school for status

## Common Questions

### Q: Why don't I see the Exams tab?
**A:** Early Childhood Development (ECD) schools don't use this feature. If you believe this is an error, contact your school.

### Q: When will exam results be available?
**A:** Check with your school. Results are typically published within 2-4 weeks of the exam.

### Q: Can I print my exam results?
**A:** Yes! Click "View Full Certificate" then use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P).

### Q: What if my results are blocked?
**A:** Some results may be blocked for administrative reasons. Contact your school for more information.

### Q: How long are results kept?
**A:** Results are permanently stored and always available to view.

### Q: Can I see past years' results?
**A:** Yes! The card shows all your published exam results, sorted by most recent first.

## Getting Help

If you experience issues:

1. **Check your internet connection**
2. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Cmd+R)
3. **Clear browser cache** (try private/incognito mode)
4. **Contact your school** if issues persist
